About Me

I am currently a third year Games Programmer, focusing primarily in Unity and C#. I began my programming journey in 2022 with little experience and within a few years at university, I developed my skills and have been happy exploring my passion for creating games.

My area of focus is usually character controllers, as I find the process of prototyping a design and iterating on it until it is of the quality I desire to be especially satisfying. Within larger projects, I have also explored level design, thinking beyond the code and instead considering the user experience in a more holistic way.
