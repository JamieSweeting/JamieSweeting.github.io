Crab Tank

An crab-based FPS aim trainer

Overview

On a last minute whim, I participated in Global Gam Jam 2025 as part of small team, creating an underwater aim trainer where you play as a crab and unlock upgrades for your gun - a short, replayable experience.

Joining late, my main contributions included the combo system, which rewarded the player for continuously landing shots under time pressure, along with creating a versatile ammo/reloading system to allow for the upgrades to enhance the player experience.
